About

101 Rams football and Cheer are a 501(c)3 non-profit corporation that was established in 2014. Our home games are played at Westlake High School typically on Saturdays. Our practices start beginning of August are held at Westlake High School or Westlake YMCA fields, typically 3-4 days per week for 2-3 hours per day. Playoffs typically end in October. Players must commit to attending all practices and games throughout the season, August - October.

We play in the Southern California Conference of Pop Warner, playing teams in the South Bay, Los Angeles and Ventura Counties. There are between 8-10 games played per season. Away games could be in any of these cities. Younger kids typically play in the morning and older kids in the afternoon.

Our organization is run entirely by volunteers. All the members of the Board of Directors are volunteers, and are not compensated for the hours they work throughout the year to keep this program going. All our coaches are also volunteers who put in many hours on and off the practice fields each season. Our organization is funded through registration fees, sponsors, and fundraisers. All funds are put back into the program to pay expenses and make improvements. Some of the expenses we incur each year include repair and/or replacement of personal safety equipment and uniforms for all youth athletes, field usage fees, insurance, Pop Warner league membership dues, merchandise for resale, referees, post season play, end-of-year trophies and awards, and other miscellaneous administrative expenses.

Each year we strive to improve and nurture this program, thus ensuring its continued existence and availability for the community. We appreciate the support and enthusiasm of all our volunteers, sponsors, and parents.

Cheer

Welcome to the 101 Rams Youth Cheer Page

Our program is a non-competitive spirit-based program for girls in Kindergarten through 8th grade (ages 5-13). Our emphasis is on teaching the girls cheers, stunts and dance routines for sideline and half-time performances. We work closely with the Westlake High School Cheerleaders.

We have 5 Cheer Divisions. Squad placement is based on age and enrollment. Squad placement is emailed by the first practice.
